About Us


Africa Youth for Peace and Development (AYPAD) is a non-governmental, non-political, non-religious, non-partisan organization. Our mission is to promote peace and development through Africaâ€™s youth. We aim to transform the lives of the disadvantaged and empower the marginalized, forging a path to sustainable peace and prosperity on the African continent.

AYPAD founded in 1995 is a registered non-profit charity with the government of Sierra Leone, since 2000. We currently have 9 chapters and operate in 30 contries across the African continent, and are continuing to grow with the help of a dedicated local volunteers. Dedicated volunteers whom organize development projects, conferences, workshops and training programs, thereby creating a culture of peace and nonviolence. The organization relies solely on charitable contributions which go directly to our projects as none or our staff, leadership team included, are salaried.
