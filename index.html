Africa Youth for Peace and Development

AYPAD is a youth serving, empowerment, and advocacy movement with a mission and vision of ensuring peace and development among youth around the world through enabling empowerment activities such as capacity building and leadership training, formal and non-formal education, social soft skills activities, environmental health, IT skills and sporting activities.

Our Story

Since 2000

Micro-Credit Project

The Micro-credit Project main thrust is to effect a revolving micro-credit scheme for women members, of AYPAD, as a means of enchaining their economic empowerment.

Basic Education

The Street Children Program aims to give youth all the basic knowledge that anyone needs to live and have a future in our modern times.

Sports Program

The Sports program was created to engage youth into peaceful and peace-promoting activities which build skills and promotes physical, spiritual, and mental well-being.

Arts and Crafts Project

The Arts & Crafts program aims to engage youth and children in art activities to promote peace and develop their talents

Tree Planting

The Tree Planting program works to educate people on the importance to keep our environment clean, reduce carbon emissions, engage in afforestation and the need to care for the world we live.

Cultural Project

The Cultural program aims to engage youth and children in cultural activities to promote peace and develop their talents providing a context outside formal institutions where youth can come together to better understand each other and their culture.

Hockey Project

The Hockey program was created to engage youth into peaceful and peace-promoting activities building and promoting physical, spiritual, and mental well-being.

Skills Training Center

The Skills Training program aims to engage youth and children in activities such as capacity building and leadership training, formal and non-formal education, social soft skills activities, and IT skills.
